How Social Publisher™ Works

WBN Social Publisher™ is built on a simple idea: one story becomes many platform-ready social posts. Instead of manually rewriting every update for every platform, you write a master post once, and Social Publisher adapts it for each account and network, keeping your brand voice consistent while respecting each platform's character limits, link formats, and media rules.

01
Connect Your Accounts
Add your Bluesky accounts in the Accounts tab. Each account carries its own handle, default hashtags, call-to-action, and time zone. In Phase 2, OAuth authentication will connect directly to the Bluesky AT Protocol.
02
Write in the Composer
Open the Composer and select a target account. Write your master post text. The live character counter enforces Bluesky's 300-character limit. Add a link and optional image. A real-time Bluesky preview card shows exactly how your post will appear.
03
Save, Schedule, or Publish
Save a draft to continue later, schedule for a specific time, or publish directly (live in Phase 2). All posts enter the Queue where you can track their status from Draft through to Published or Failed.
04
Track on the Calendar
The weekly Calendar view shows all scheduled and published posts across accounts. Use it to spot gaps in your publishing rhythm and keep your editorial calendar full.
05
Review Analytics
Phase 1 Analytics tracks your internal publishing metrics: total posts, scheduled, published, and failed counts by account. Platform-native engagement analytics (likes, reposts, impressions) arrive in Phase 2 with live Bluesky connection.
WBN Social Publisher™
The Social Publishing Layer of WBN Cast OS™

WBN Social Publisher™ is a first-party application built on top of the WBN Operating Platform™. It provides WBN Cast Network with a unified, editorial-grade workflow for distributing stories across social platforms — starting with Bluesky and expanding to additional networks in future phases. It is not a general-purpose social media tool. It is purpose-built for the WBN Cast editorial ecosystem.
