How Social Publisher™ Works
WBN Global Edition — This instance of WBN Social Publisher™ is dedicated to the WBN Global editorial team. It connects directly to your Bluesky accounts via the AT Protocol, with all credentials secured in Cloudflare Workers. Posts are stored in Supabase and scheduled posts fire automatically via Cloudflare Cron Triggers — even when no browser tab is open.
1. Connect Accounts
Go to Accounts tab. Click Connect Account. Enter your Bluesky handle and an App Password generated in Bluesky Settings → App Passwords. Credentials are validated against the AT Protocol and stored securely in Cloudflare.
2. Compose
Write your post, attach up to 4 images, add a link and hashtags. The live preview shows exactly how it will appear on Bluesky. The character counter enforces the 300-character limit in real time.
3. Publish or Schedule
Hit Publish Now to post immediately — you'll be prompted for your App Password to authorise the post. Or schedule for a future date and time. Cloudflare Cron fires scheduled posts automatically every minute.
4. Track
The Queue shows every post's status from Draft through to Published or Failed. The Calendar gives a weekly view across all accounts. The Activity Log records every event in real time from Supabase.

Architecture
All data lives in Supabase PostgreSQL. All API calls go through the Cloudflare Worker at wbn-sp-api.gmoen.workers.dev. No secrets ever touch the browser. App passwords are used per publish action and never stored in the UI.
Connecting Accounts
Go to Accounts → Connect Account. You need a Bluesky App Password, not your main password. Generate one in Bluesky Settings → App Passwords. Name it wbn-social-publisher. Each account is validated against the AT Protocol before saving.
Publishing Flow
Compose → Save Draft → Queue → Publish Now or Schedule. Publish Now prompts for your App Password each time for security. Scheduled posts fire via Cloudflare Cron every minute — no browser tab needed.
Images
Up to 4 images per post. JPEG, PNG, WEBP, GIF. Max 5MB each. Images preview instantly in the composer. On publish, they are uploaded to Bluesky via uploadBlob before the post is created.
Hub & Spoke
This is the WBN Global spoke. WBN Corp (the hub) can push posts to this edition. Corp-pushed posts appear in the Queue as corp_push items for review before publishing.
